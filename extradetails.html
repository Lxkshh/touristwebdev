Explore Lonavla

Lonavla is a popular hill station and tourist destination in Maharashtra, India, known for its scenic beauty, lush greenery, and pleasant climate. Here are some famous places to visit in Lonavla:

1]Tiger's Leap (Tiger Point) 2]Bhushi Dam 3]Rajmachi Point 4]Karla Caves 5]Lohagad Fort 6]Ryewood Park 7]Wax Museum 8]Kune Waterfalls 9]Duke's Nose 10]Lonavla Lake (Monsoon Lake)

Nestled amidst the majestic Sahyadri mountain range in Maharashtra, Lonavla beckons with its enchanting beauty and serene ambiance. This picturesque hill station boasts a landscape adorned with rolling hills, verdant valleys, and cascading waterfalls, creating a mesmerizing setting that captivates visitors throughout the year. The monsoon season, in particular, transforms Lonavla into a lush green paradise, as numerous waterfalls come to life, including the renowned Kune Falls and Bhushi Dam Waterfall. These natural wonders draw tourists and nature enthusiasts eager to witness their majestic beauty and experience the rejuvenating power of nature's bounty.
